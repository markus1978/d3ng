To allow you to visualize the relationships between data and its different visualizations, d3ng allows you to connect individual simple visualizations via user interactions. D3ng uses selections and selection groups to connect different representations of the same data-points. This kind of visual analysis task is known as brushing and linking.

D3ng support 4 different types of possible chart combinations, each employing brushing and linking in a different manner.
